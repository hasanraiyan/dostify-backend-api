# Dostify - Account & Data Deletion Request

**Last Updated:** April 3, 2026

---

## Request Account Deletion

If you wish to delete your **Dostify** account and all associated data, you have two options:

### Option 1: Delete from Within the App (Recommended)

1. Open the **Dostify** app on your device
2. Navigate to your **Profile Settings**
3. Tap on **Delete Account**
4. Enter your password to verify your identity
5. Confirm the deletion

This is the fastest way to delete your account. Your account and all associated data will be permanently deleted immediately.

### Option 2: Request Deletion via Email

If you no longer have access to the app, you can request account deletion by emailing us at:

**raiyanhasan2006@gmail.com**

Please include the following information in your email:
- The email address associated with your Dostify account
- A clear request to delete your account and all associated data

We will process your request within **30 days** of receiving it.

---

## What Data Is Deleted

When you request account deletion, the following data will be **permanently deleted** from our servers:

| Data Type | Description | Status |
|-----------|-------------|--------|
| **User Profile** | Your name, email, avatar, and account credentials | Deleted |
| **Chat Messages** | All conversations with our AI assistant (your messages and AI responses) | Deleted |
| **Tasks** | All task titles, descriptions, dates, times, categories, priorities, and completion status | Deleted |
| **Journal Entries** | All journal content, titles, and mood indicators | Deleted |
| **Authentication Account** | Your Supabase Auth account and session tokens | Deleted |
| **App Usage Data** | Experience points (XP), level, credit balance, onboarding status, theme preference | Deleted |

---

## What Happens After Deletion

- Your account will be **permanently deleted** and cannot be recovered
- All data associated with your account will be removed from our databases
- You will no longer be able to access any Dostify features or content associated with your account

**This action is irreversible.**

---

## Third-Party Data

Dostify uses **Supabase** as its backend service provider. When your account is deleted, we ensure that your data is also removed from Supabase's systems. For more information, please refer to [Supabase's Privacy Policy](https://supabase.com/privacy).

We do not share your personal data with any third-party marketing or advertising services.
